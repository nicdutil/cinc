Infocinc: a new company

Infocinc is a company in the metropolitan area of Montreal.

Our services and consulting are varied: website design, optimization of websites, web scraping and data modeling...

Infocinc always strive to infuse quality and intelligence in our services.

We are forward thinkers and use the best technology to create innovative solutions for our customers.

Our services:
1
WEBSITES

Website Design

Infocinc is on the cutting edge of web technologies: responsive design, NodeJs http servers, Facebook and Google+ integration, rich snippets and more.

We also offer to track the behavior of your users using Google Analytics and custom-made scripts.

Responsive design is Google's recommended configuration for smartphone-optimized websites.

Mobile Optimization

A bad mobile experience makes users less likely to engage with your company.

Infocinc improves your website's image and the layout of its content on mobile devices and tablet computers.

According to a survey by Google, 67% of mobile users are more likely to buy products or services on a mobile-friendly site.

MINING

Web Scraping

A scraper is a small computer program that extracts information from websites.

Infocinc offers its expertise with data integration solutions such as Kapow Katalyst. We design robots and automate their execution for your data extraction jobs.

This software is used by many corporations in the financial sector and the automobile industry.

Data Analysis

It is important to interpret business data correctly. Infocinc offers its expertise with the KDB+ columnar database and its proprietary language Q to build you a suite of analytics.

KDB+ and Q are widely used by the financial sector and other industries to process in realtime billions of records.

VISUALIZATION

Custom Dashboards

Data visualization is essential to establish trends and identify explanatory models (e.g. mixture of Gaussians, decision trees, Bayesian nets).

Infocinc has expertise in web technologies and modeling techniques (e.g. logistic regression, neural nets, clustering). We offer to build you a web application for viewing your data from any of your devices.

Excel Automation

An alternative to visualizing your data on the web, use the power and maturity of Excel for building intelligent reports based on the data you carefully collected.

Infocinc offers automation of your reports using VBA macros and Excel's configurable external data connections.

CONSULTATION

Technical Assistance

Do you need to clarify certain technical issues about your next project ? We offer help to resolve your technical problems: software bugs, system crashes, viruses, choice of hardware etc.

Our experience in problem solving allows us to approach less familiar areas of knowledge with confidence. Do not hesitate to contact us for help with your current technical difficulties. Infocinc can build custom software solutions if the situation arises.
