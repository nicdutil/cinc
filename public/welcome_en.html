We offer technology services to companies

Infocinc is a company in the metropolitan area of Montreal offering various technology and consulting services. We specialize in responsive web design, optimization of websites for smartphones and tablet computers, web scraping, data modeling and visualization tools. Infocinc always strive to infuse quality and intelligence in our services. We are forward thinkers and use the best technology to create innovative solutions for our customers.

Here's a quick tour of our current services:
1
WEBSITES

Website Design

Infocinc is on the cutting edge of web technologies: responsive design, NodeJs http servers, Facebook and Google+ integration, rich snippets and more. We also offer to track the behavior of your users using Google Analytics and custom-made scripts. Responsive design is Google's recommended configuration for smartphone-optimized websites.

Website Re-Design

Using responsive design techniques, Infocinc improves your website's image and the layout of its content on mobile devices and tablet computers. According to a survey by Google, more than 50% of users said that a bad mobile experience made them less likely to engage with a company. More than 67% of mobile users are more likely to buy products or services on a mobile-friendly site.

MINING

Web Scraping

A scraper is a small computer program that extracts information from websites. The recovered data can then be stored on a database for later analysis. Infocinc offers its expertise with data integration solutions such as Kapow Katalyst. This software is used by many corporations in the financial sector and the automobile industry.

Data Analysis

It is important to interpret business data correctly. Infocinc offers its expertise with the KDB+ columnar database and its proprietary language Q to build you a suite of analytics. KDB+ and Q are widely used by the financial sector and other industries to process in realtime billions of records.

VISUALIZATION

Custom Dashboards

Data visualization is essential to establish trends and identify explanatory models (e.g. mixture of Gaussians, decision trees, Bayesian nets). Infocinc has expertise in web technologies and modeling techniques (e.g. logistic regression, neural nets, clustering). We offer to build you a web application for viewing your data from any of your devices.

Excel Automation

An alternative to visualizing your data on the web, use the power and maturity of Excel for building intelligent reports based on the data you carefully collected. Infocinc offers automation of your reports using VBA macros and Excel's configurable external data connections.

CONSULTATION

Problem Solving

Do you need to clarify certain technical issues about your next project ? We offer help to resolve your technical problems: software bugs, system crashes, viruses, choice of hardware etc. Our experience in problem solving allows us to approach less familiar areas of knowledge with confidence. Do not hesitate to contact us for help with your current technical difficulties. Infocinc can build custom software solutions if the situation arises.
