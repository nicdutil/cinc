We offer technology services to companies

Infocinc is a small enterprise from the region of Montreal specializing in responsive web design, data mining and visualization. No matter what the project is about, we strive to infuse quality and intelligence in our services. We are forward thinkers and pushes ourselves to create innovative solutions for our customers.

Here's a quick tour of our current services:
1
WEB

Responsive Web Design

We create websites that adapts their visual, textual and interactive features according to the user's device characteristics. No need for an additional mobile domain to broadcast your content on Androids or iPhones. Responsive web design has you covered.

Website Upgrade

We rethink and refactor existing websites to upgrade you with the latest technologies available: google maps v3.0, better YouTube api integration, open graph/structured data tags, responsive features, tag management, etc..

MINING

Web Content Extraction

We design, build and automate programmable robots that will connect to websites and scrape web content (i.e. html tables, excel spreadsheets, unstructured data).

Data Mining

We can extract the intelligence out of your data you carefully collected using a series of complex SQL queries, regression algorithms, PCA and other unsupervised learning techniques.

VISUALISATION

Custom Dashboards

To help you understand what your data are saying to you, we build responsive web apps that contain interactive charts, tables and real-time data updating.

Excel Automation

Reduce your amount of repetitive and manual data entry work. We can build custom macros to automate the process of refreshing and displaying your data.

Our approach is scientific

We don't solely rely on our previous experiences to assure us of success. Our background in computer science research taught us to take a more formal approach to our projects. We like to engineer solutions. Our mission is to provide you with technology services based on verifiable facts, that follows precise specifications and meet the requirements established by our clients.
